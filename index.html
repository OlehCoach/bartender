How to Play Chicken Road

Follow these steps to start playing Chicken Road crash game online.

🟩 Block 1 — Place Your Bet

Place Your Bet
Choose your stake before the round starts. Chicken Road is a high-volatility crash game, so responsible bankroll management is essential.

🟨 Block 2 — Watch the Multiplier

Watch the Multiplier Grow
As the chicken moves forward, the multiplier increases in real time. The longer you stay in the round, the higher the potential payout — and the higher the risk.

🟥 Block 3 — Cash Out Before the Crash

Cash Out in Time
Click the Cash Out button before the crash occurs. If the crash happens first, the bet is lost.

🟦 Block 4 — Select Difficulty Mode

Choose a Difficulty Mode
Chicken Road offers multiple difficulty levels — Easy, Medium, Hard, and Hardcore. Each mode affects crash frequency and maximum multiplier potential.

🟪 Block 5 — Understand the Risk

Understand the Risk
Chicken Road is based on timing and decision-making. Results vary, and no strategy guarantees profit.

🟫 Block 6 — Play Responsibly

Play Responsibly
Set limits and play for entertainment. Crash games can be fast-paced and unpredictable.
